About CureSeeker

CureSeeker helps RuneScape players quickly identify animal diseases in Player-Owned Farms by matching examination text to disease symptoms. Based on the official RuneScape Wiki Disease page.

How to Use

  1. Install Alt1 from https://runeapps.org/alt1
  2. Click the "Add App" button in the top right of this page
  3. Grant pixel reading permissions when prompted
  4. Examine diseased animals in RuneScape
  5. The app will match examination text to identify the disease
